Aerospace Timeline

1903 - Wright Flyer completes the first powered flight.
1947 - Bell X-1 breaks the sound barrier.
1969 - Apollo 11 lands humans on the Moon.
1981 - Space Shuttle begins reusable spacecraft missions.
Today - AI, drones, and reusable rockets change aerospace engineering.
